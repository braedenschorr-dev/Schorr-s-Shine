About Schorr Shine

Hi, I'm Braeden.
This is my story.

I'm a 20-year-old business owner and college student here in the Capital Region. Detailing didn't start as a business for me — it started as something I did for myself. I've always loved taking care of my own vehicle, and there was something about putting in the hours and then stepping back to see the finished result that never got old. That feeling was always worth the work.

Eventually, other people started noticing. Friends and family kept asking me the same question: "Why don't you make a business out of this?" For a while I brushed it off. Then one day I stopped wondering and just did it. That's how Schorr Shine was born.

"Every car I detail carries my name on it — so I don't consider the job done until it's something I'd be proud to show off."

When you book with Schorr Shine, you're not dealing with a big chain or a call center. You're working directly with me — someone who genuinely cares how your car turns out, because my name is literally on the business. I treat every vehicle like it's my own, and I bring the same obsessive attention to your interior that I've always brought to mine.

Thanks for taking the time to learn a little about me — and for supporting a young, local business. I hope to see you (and your car) soon.

— Braeden

Braeden — Founder, Schorr Shine
